About

From dropping beats
to dropping punchlines.

Born in Jamshedpur, raised across four states, and eventually planted in the DMV — Toby Rao spent his early career doing everything the traditional path demanded. Engineering? Checked. MBA? Double-checked. Aunties satisfied? Temporarily.

Then he discovered the real talent: walking into any room on earth and knowing, within 30 seconds, exactly what would make it laugh. Fluent in seven languages and two decades of South Asian weddings, Toby turned a lifetime of cultural code-switching into a comedy superpower.

Seven years as an elite DMV DJ. Radio Zindagi host. Wedding MC for hundreds of events. Now, he does it with a mic instead of a mixer — and the crowd never notices the difference. They just can't stop laughing.
