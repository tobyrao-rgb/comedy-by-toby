The Story

From dropping beats to dropping punchlines.

"Always the funniest person in the room. Now it's official."

Born and raised in Jamshedpur — a melting pot of cultures. College in Chennai and Bangalore. Lived across 4 states. Debate trophies from school and college. Eleven years running nightclubs and destination weddings on cruise ships. Wedding MC. Radio Zindagi host. Seven years as a DJ in the DMV.

Traveled across the globe. A perfect balance of North and South India. Speaks 7 languages. Knows the culture and what punches to pull. Because culture is the foundation of real connection.

— Toby Rao
